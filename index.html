    About Me

    My name is Giulia, and my journey towards becoming a birthkeeper started in the early days of my childhood. Surrounded by midwives like my mother, I always felt a deep connection to the world of maternal wellness. Over time, I became keenly aware of a significant gap in 1:1 care for expectant mothers, especially within hospital settings. This insight ignited a desire within me to provide the care, familiarity, stability, and support that every woman deserves during this transformative period.

    As an holistic therapist, I initially specialized in pregnancy massage. During my interactions with hundreds of women, I realized there was a wide spectrum of needs and fears that often went unaddressed. Witnessing my best friend's struggle with postpartum loneliness and depression further highlighted the importance of support during and after childbirth.

    I believe in a comprehensive approach to wellness. By combining different techniques such as massage therapy, reflexology, aromatherapy, Bach flowers, meditation, relaxation, and visualization, I aim to ensure the physical, mental, and spiritual well-being of my clients.

    When it comes to breastfeeding, I understand that every mother-baby pair is unique. By providing tailored support and reassurance, I hope to dispel fears, uncertainties, and misconceptions that often surround breastfeeding. My mission is to create a safe space where mothers and babies can build their unique relationships with breastfeeding without guilt or pressure.

    In my role as a birthkeeper, I advocate for the humanization of the birth experience. I strive to help mothers navigate their journeys without external pressures and expectations, and to make informed decisions that feel right for them. I see childbirth as a sacred time in a woman's life that should be cherished and experienced positively, and I feel honored to be a part of it.

    I bring to my practice a multifaceted education that harmoniously integrates the holistic approach with the medicalization of birth. With fluency in English and Italian and experience with both healthcare systems, I am uniquely equipped to serve a diverse range of mothers-to-be.

    With your trust and my commitment, together we can transform your pregnancy journey into a beautiful and empowering experience.

    I look forward to supporting you on your journey.

    Pregnancy

    An Emotional Rollercoaster

    Pregnancy, though filled with special moments, is an intense experience that can bring about a range of emotions. These can include fear, loss, and change, especially as the birth approaches and uncertainties about labor and the baby's health arise. The emotional burden, along with the physical strain of carrying a child, can make this period overwhelming.

    How can I help?

    • Pregnancy Massage
    • Birth-planning
    • Support making informed choices
    Birth

    A Challenging Gift

    Despite the difficulties, pregnancy provides a unique opportunity for personal growth. Each person's experiences, history, traumas, and triumphs shape them, and their babies are influenced by these factors too. This makes every birth unique, negating any standardizations or expectations that society may impose.

    How can I help?

    • On Call Labour Support
    • Pain Management Techniques
    • Emotional and physical support to you and your family
    • Birthing Partner
    Post Natal

    Creating Your Own Journey

    Embracing this uniqueness allows one to create their own pregnancy journey. The ability to discern what feels right and to forge your own path is crucial in navigating through this period. You are capable and equipped to support your baby in navigating their new world.
